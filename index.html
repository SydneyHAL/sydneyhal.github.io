--- layout: home author_profile: false title: Sydney Human Augmentation Lab excerpt: > The Sydney Human Augmentation Lab (Sydney HAL) designs and evaluates technologies that extend human perception, action, and capability through rigorous, human-centred research. --- Sydney HAL is a research lab based at the University of Technology Sydney, focused on the design, evaluation, and deployment of human augmentation technologies. Our work spans extended reality, haptics, sensing, and assistive systems, with an emphasis on real-world use, embodied interaction, and responsible innovation. We collaborate closely with industry, clinicians, and community partners to ensure that augmentation technologies are effective, interpretable, and aligned with human needs.
